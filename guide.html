    This is a sample design for a video sharing platform designed by Team BrightFuture. The SEARCH page gives its users the option of searching a video. Moreover, on the HOMEPAGE, the user can select a video from a grid of all available videos. Clicking on one, the user is taken to a VIDEO PAGE where they can watch the video without disturbance.

    This project was completed by Will, Raheem, and Athar for ProjectFunction Wave 7, 2022.
